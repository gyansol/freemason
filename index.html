CRAFTING THE FUTURE

A JOURNEY THROUGH DIGITAL FREEMASONRY AND INNOVATION

Embark on a visionary odyssey exploring the nexus of digital Freemasonry and innovation, shaping the future through craft and creativity.
PHASE 1

World Union of Sovereign Lodges ‘Libertas 5775’

In January 28, 2021, in Zagreb, the World Union of Sovereign Lodges ‘Libertas 5775’ was founded. Three months later, on March 19, in Sarajevo, three Grand Lodges with other obediences from Croatia, Serbia, Hungary, and North Macedonia signed the Declaration of the Foundation and Sovereignty, thereby committing themselves to preserving the Masonic system, ancient traditions, and teachings established in 1775 under the so-called Drašković Observance, and introducing the rules and principles upon which this Union is based.

PHASE 2

Ofiicial Digital Freemasonry Community & "Declaration of Foundations and Fundamental Principles".

On March 19, 2021, the history of Freemasonry witnessed an event that will be engraved on its pages. This was the moment when the digital age became woven into the fabric of Freemasonry, triggering the next stage of evolution. That day marks the beginning of the official move towards Digital Freemasonry with ODFC (Official Digital Freemasonry Community. But that was not just the beginning. One year later, on March 19, 2022, the world of Freemasonry welcomed the "Declaration of Foundations and Fundamental Principles" - a document that marked the official beginning of Digital Freemasonry.

PHASE 3

1st Digital Freemasonry Summit

On March 18 and 19, the 1st Digital Freemasonry Summit was organized in Zagreb by the World Union Libertas 5775 and ODFC. The summit focused on presenting and introducing Masonic organizations to blockchain technology and digital tools that will be introduced in the coming period as official instruments in Digital Freemasonry, as well as in speculative Freemasonry. The summit was attended by obediences from the region, as well as distinguished guests from the respected online magazine dedicated to Freemasonry, The Square Magazine, which also served as the official media sponsor of the event.

PHASE 4

Grand Lodge ‘’Founders of Digital Freemasonry’’

On March 19, 2024 the World Union Libertas 5775 granted permission for the establishment of a special research Grand Lodge that will focus on studying and researching blockchain technology and artificial intelligence, as well as on how to implement these tools in the most appropriate way, in the spirit of Freemasonry, for the benefit of the entire Masonic community worldwide.

PHASE 5

2nd Digital Freemasonry Summit Belgrade and dApp Masonic Passport

On March 19, 2025, the 2nd Digital Freemasonry Summit was held in Belgrade, where the decentralized and first Masonic application based on blockchain technology, the dApp Masonic Passport, was presented. Other upcoming tools of Digital Freemasonry were also announced, including the Official Digital Freemasonry Token (ODFT), Official Digital Freemasonry NFT Marketplace and the ultimate goal, MasonicVerse.

PHASE 6

Official Digital Freemasonry Token Libertas 5775

in 2026, Official Digital Freemasonry Token (ODFT) also known as Libertas 5775 – is a stable digital token pegged to the value of 1 US dollar (USD), developed as the official currency within the ecosystem of Digital Freemasonry. It is not available on public exchanges but exclusively to verified Community members, aiming to create a secure, decentralized, and self-sustaining economic system.

PHASE 7

Official Digital Freemasonry NFT Marketplace
